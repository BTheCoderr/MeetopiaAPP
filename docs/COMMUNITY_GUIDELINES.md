# Meetopia Community Guidelines

Meetopia is for **adults 18+** who want real chemistry through live video — dating, new friends, and local meetups. Help keep it respectful and safe.

## Be yourself (safely)

- Use accurate age and profile information.
- Do not impersonate others or use stolen photos.
- Keep prompts friendly and appropriate for a public dating/meeting context.

## Respect boundaries

- No harassment, hate speech, threats, or bullying.
- Stop if someone asks to end the call or chat.
- Mutual Vibe is required before text chat — respect that flow.

## No illegal or harmful content

- No nudity or sexual content in violation of our policies or law.
- No spam, scams, or solicitation for money or external payment apps.
- No sharing of others’ private information (doxxing).

## Adults only

- You must be **18 or older** to use Meetopia.
- If you believe someone is underage, **Report → Underage user** and leave the chat immediately.

## Safety tools

- **Report** — flag behavior for review.
- **Block** — stop matching with that user for your session (and locally stored).
- **Leave** — exit any Chemistry Check at any time.

## Enforcement

Reports are reviewed by our team (MVP: logged for manual review). Repeat or severe violations may result in **warnings, suspension, or permanent bans** when account systems are live.

We do **not** currently use AI moderation or automatic video blur. Stay alert and use Report/Block if something feels wrong.
